GSA Access Card Credentialing Progress Report

As of December 1, 2017

As of December 1, 2017, GSA Identity, Credential, and Access Management (ICAM) Division has credentialed and issued GSA Access Cards to:

  • Over 72% of the total GSA Personnel
  • Over 99% of employees; and
  • Over 63% of our contractors

Toward Better Security

The 2004 HSPD-12 directive seeks to establish universal identification standards. As these standards evolve, GSA is at the forefront of offering efficient, innovative HSPD-12 and other OMB policy compliant products and services.

For USAccess
Managed Services Offices
hspd12.security@gsa.gov

GSA’s USAccess program offers federal agencies a shared service that simplifies the process of procuring and maintaining an enterprisewide compliant Personal Identity Verification (PIV) credential solution.
